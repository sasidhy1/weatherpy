Humidity

The scatter plot of Latitude vs. Humidity seems to have a less obvious pattern with a wider spread of data, resembling an upside-down dome. We can assume that the humidity in cities is not predominately affected by their latitudes, but by some other lurking variables. From this scatter plot, we can at least tell that there is less humidity away from the equator and closer to the north and south poles. i.e. Arctic climate zones are ice-covered year round and extremely dry.
