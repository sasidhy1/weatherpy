Cloudiness

The scatter plot of Latitude vs. Cloudiness seems to have no obvious pattern with a suspicious spread of data. We cannot definitively say that the spread is random, and that latitude has no effect on cloudiness because of the concentration of cities at the lower and upper ends of the graph. From this scatter plot, we can see there are clusters of cities across all latitudes centered around the 0 and 90 percentages of reported cloudiness.
